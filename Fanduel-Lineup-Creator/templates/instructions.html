Fanduel Lineup Optimizer Instructions

Follow these 4 easy steps to use the fanduel lineup optimizer and retrieve an optimal lineup. When you are done following the steps below, click this link to use the optimizer.

Use Fanduel Optimizer

Step 1:

First visit the Fanduel website and choose an NBA contest you wish to participate in. You must have a registered valid account on fanduel to complete this step.

Step 2:

Visit the Upcoming tab, find the slate you want to edit and select 'CSV Edit. Then download the csv to your computer.'

Step 3:

After downloading your csv, select the full lineup including all rows from Player ID to Injury Indicator. Open a new excel worksheet and copy paste the full lineup into the new worksheet. Finally save the worksheet as a csv.

Step 4:

After you have obtained your full lineup for the contest, return to the optimizer page and insert your csv file. Then your good to go!
